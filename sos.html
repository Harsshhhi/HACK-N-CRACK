SOS - Send Emergency Message

Click the button below to send an SOS message to your emergency contact.

How to Use SOS

  • Ensure your emergency contact number is correct.
  • Click the "Send SOS" button to open your SMS app.
  • The message "SOS: I need help! Please assist me immediately." will be pre-filled.
  • Press send in your SMS app to alert your contact.
