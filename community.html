Auto Rickshaw Safety in Mumbai

Walking Home in Delhi

Anjali Gupta - May 9, 2025

I avoid shortcuts through narrow lanes in Delhi, especially after dark. Stick to main roads and use a pepper spray if needed.

Using Public Buses in Bangalore

Meena Rao - May 8, 2025

In Bangalore, I always board buses from well-lit stops and sit near the conductor. It feels safer during late hours.

Market Safety in Kolkata

Shalini Das - May 7, 2025

When shopping in crowded markets like New Market, keep your bag close and avoid displaying cash. I also stay with a group of friends.
