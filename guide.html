How to Use Here Am

Getting Started

  • Create an account by signing up with your phone number.
  • Login using the one-time password (OTP) sent to your mobile.
  • Explore the homepage to access all safety features.

Using Emergency Features

  • Navigate to the Emergency page to find quick-dial numbers for police, ambulance, and helplines.
  • Use the SOS feature to send an immediate alert to your emergency contact.

Traveling Safely

  • Use the Safe Path feature to find recommended routes to your destination.
  • Follow our safety tips while traveling, such as staying in well-lit areas.

Staying Connected

  • Keep your phone charged and the Here Am website accessible at all times.
  • Share your location with trusted contacts using the SOS feature if needed.
